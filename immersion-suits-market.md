<p><ul><li>Insulated Immersion Suits</li><li>Un-Insulated Immersion Suits</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The immersion suits market is divided into two main types: insulated and un-insulated immersion suits. Insulated immersion suits provide thermal protection, designed to retain body heat in cold water environments, making them suitable for extreme conditions and prolonged exposure. Un-insulated immersion suits, on the other hand, offer buoyancy and basic protection without significant thermal insulation, primarily used in warmer waters or short-duration emergencies. Both types play a vital role in marine safety and survival during maritime emergencies.</p></p>
