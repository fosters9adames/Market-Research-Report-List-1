<p><p>The aquarium filter market is divided into two primary segments: freshwater and saltwater filters. In contrast, saltwater filters cater to marine environments, often incorporating advanced technologies like protein skimmers and UV sterilizers to handle the unique challenges of saltwater chemistry and high bio-loads. Both markets emphasize effective filtration, but their technologies reflect the specific needs of the aquatic habitats.</p></p>
