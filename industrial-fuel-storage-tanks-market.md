<p><ul><li>Single Wall Fuel Storage Tanks</li><li>Double-Wall Fuel Storage Tanks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial fuel storage tanks market primarily consists of single-wall and double-wall fuel storage tanks. Single-wall tanks are simpler in design, featuring a single layer of steel or other materials, making them cost-effective but potentially less secure against leaks. In contrast, double-wall tanks incorporate a secondary containment layer, providing enhanced safety by minimizing the risk of spillage and environmental contamination. This feature makes double-wall tanks more suitable for industries with stringent regulatory requirements and higher safety standards.</p></p>
