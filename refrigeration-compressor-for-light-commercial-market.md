<p><p>In the light commercial refrigeration market, several compressor types cater to specific cooling needs. Screw compressors offer high efficiency for larger applications, handling significant cooling loads. Scroll compressors are reliable and energy-efficient, suitable for medium capacities. Lastly, centrifugal compressors excel in large systems due to their high flow rates and capability to provide extensive cooling in commercial settings.</p></p>
