<p><p>Directional sign market types include wall-mounted and floor-standing signs, both serving to guide individuals and enhance navigation in various environments. Wall-mounted signs are affixed to surfaces, making them ideal for tight spaces or areas with limited floor area, providing clear visibility at eye level. Conversely, floor-standing signs are independent structures that can be positioned anywhere, often used in lobbies, events, or large buildings, offering flexibility in placement while ensuring prominent visibility to direct foot traffic effectively.</p></p>
