<p><strong>The Coolant Temperature Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>2-Wire Coolant Temperature Sensors</li><li>1-Wire Coolant Temperature Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coolant temperature sensors market includes two main types: 2-wire and 1-wire sensors. 2-wire coolant temperature sensors utilize a simple design, consisting of two wires that connect directly to the vehicle's engine control unit, providing a straightforward and reliable connection for temperature readings. In contrast, 1-wire coolant temperature sensors feature a single wire that communicates temperature data over a bus system, allowing for reduced wiring complexity and integration with multiple sensors. Both types play a critical role in engine management for efficient vehicle operation.</p></p>
