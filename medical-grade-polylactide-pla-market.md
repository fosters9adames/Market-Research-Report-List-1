<p><strong>The Medical-grade Polylactide (PLA) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Medical L-Polylactide</li><li>Medical Racemic Polylactide</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical-grade polylactide (PLA) market comprises two primary types: Medical L-Polylactide and Medical Racemic Polylactide. Medical L-Polylactide is a pure form derived from L-lactic acid, known for its superior biocompatibility and biodegradability, making it ideal for applications like sutures and drug delivery systems. In contrast, Medical Racemic Polylactide is a copolymer of L- and D-lactic acids, offering different mechanical properties and degradation rates, suitable for various surgical implants and tissue engineering solutions. Both types are essential in advancing medical applications.</p></p>
