Colloidal rubber involves breaking down rubber into a fine liquid state, suitable for coatings and adhesives. Rubber powder, obtained through further grinding, is used in diverse applications such as automotive parts, asphalt modification, and molded goods, enhancing material properties while promoting sustainability.</p></p>
