<p><p>The relay lenses market encompasses various types, primarily focusing on ordinary spherical lenses and aspheric lenses. Ordinary spherical lenses have a uniform curvature, suitable for basic imaging applications, often utilized in low-cost solutions. In contrast, aspheric lenses feature a more complex surface profile, which minimizes optical aberrations, improving image quality in compact designs. Aspheric lenses are increasingly favored in advanced applications such as cameras and projectors, driving innovation and market growth by enhancing performance and efficiency in optical systems.</p></p>
