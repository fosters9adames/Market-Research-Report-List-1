<p><p>The solid tumors drugs market is primarily divided into two types: small molecules and biologics. Small molecules are low molecular weight compounds that can easily penetrate cells and often target specific enzymes or proteins, disrupting tumor growth. Biologics, on the other hand, are larger, complex molecules derived from living organisms, such as monoclonal antibodies or vaccines, which aim to harness the immune system to fight cancer. Both types play crucial roles in the treatment of various solid tumors, offering diverse therapeutic approaches.</p></p>
